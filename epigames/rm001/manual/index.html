Use the app to play
AVATAR status
Your avatar can be healthy or sick. When you're infected, you can spread the disease to players nearby—even if your avatar looks healthy (asymptomatic) .
Monitor the Outbreak
As you play, you'll see real-time outbreak statistics showing how the disease is spreading. You will see data about symptoms and quarantine status among other participants. Use this information to guide your strategy and protect yourself!
thE Point system
Each day between 8am–1pm , decide whether to quarantine or not and earn points based on your choice. While quarantining, you cannot get infected or infect others. Remember : If you forget to choose your quarantine status, you'll lose points for that day.

 Important : If your avatar gets sick, you'll earn fewer points each day, even if you quarantine. If your avatar dies, you'll lose all your points.
Notifications
You may receive quizzes or messages with important information about the ongoing outbreak and the daily raffle winners, so pay attention to the app's notifications!
